Japanese Curry Rice

Description

Japanese Curry Rice is a tasty dish made with rice, Japanese curry sauce with potatoes, onions and carrots, and a fried chicken cutlet on top.

It is not too spicy but is flavorful. Perfect for people who like the taste of curry but don't like the spice.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Japanese curry roux
  • Raw chicken cutlet
  • Japanese white rice
  • Egg
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Slice onions, potatoes, and carrots. Prepare Japanese white rice before cooking the curry.
  2. Poke some holes into the raw chicken to make it tender after cooking.
  3. Bread the chicken cutlet with egg, flour, and breadcrumbs.
  4. Put some oil into a pot and stir fry the onions until the edges start browning.
  5. Add potato and carrot pieces and stir it with the onions.
  6. Add water and bring the pot to a boil while removing scum with a ladle.
  7. Add Japanese curry roux and let it cook.
  8. While waiting for the curry to thicken, begin frying the breaded chicken cutlet.
  9. Once the curry thickens to a smooth, thick consistency, turn off the heat. The chicken cutlet should be done frying by now as well.
  10. On a plate, pour your curry on the side next to the rice.
  11. Place your chicken cutlet on top of the rice and curry.
  12. Enjoy~
