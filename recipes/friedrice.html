Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat.

Personally, I like to include various toppings depending on what I prefer at the time or day.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Soy sauce
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Oyster sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Ground white pepper
  • Meats/vegetables/seafood

Steps

  1. Add some oil to a pan and heat it on low heat.
  2. Cook any meats/vegetables/seafood you want to add to your fried rice.
  3. Crack some eggs and stir fry them.
  4. Add your rice to the pan and incorporate the egg/meats/veges into the rice.
  5. Start adding the powder and sauces.
  6. Adjust the seasonings to personal taste. Sesame oil is the secret to good fried rice, but don't add too much.
  7. Once done cooking the fried rice, plate the fried rice and season the fried rice with some ground white pepper.
  8. Enjoy!
